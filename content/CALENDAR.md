# Posting calendar

Cadence: **3 posts/week**, mid-morning US Eastern. Refresh + batch-render runs
Monday AM; you post from the shelf in `graphs/`. House style: no em dashes.
End each post with a question to drive comments. Rotate charts so the feed
stays varied.

## Weekly template
| Day | Slot | Chart | Notes |
|-----|------|-------|-------|
| Mon | Pace | `pace` | Always fresh; the reliable weekly beat |
| Wed | Deep-dive | rotating Tier-2 | cwe / vendor / product / rhythm / ... |
| Fri | Exploitation or novelty | `kev` or Tier-3 | Lead on exploitation, or a high-share format |

## Rotation queue (Wednesdays)
Cycle through so no topic repeats too soon:
cwe → vendor → product → rhythm → cvss_drift → time_to_kev → id_burn → (repeat)

## Off-schedule triggers
- **Milestone** — fire a milestone card when a round number lands (e.g. all-time
  catalog crosses 350,000; H1 crosses 40,000).
- **Big KEV week** — an unusually large batch of KEV additions warrants a same-day post.
