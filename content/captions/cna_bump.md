## LinkedIn / Mastodon
```
For most of the CVE program's history, one organization published the most CVEs almost every month: MITRE, the nonprofit that runs the program itself.

That is no longer true. GitHub has been the number one CVE issuer every month of 2026, while MITRE has slid to around number 7.

This chart is the monthly rank of every CVE Numbering Authority over the last two years. Watch the two lines trade places: GitHub in red climbing from 7th to 1st, MITRE in navy falling from the top to 7th.

To be clear, MITRE has not stopped doing its job, and this is not about buggier software. GitHub publishes CVEs for the open-source packages in its ecosystem, at a scale that now outpaces even the program's own operator. Cataloging vulnerabilities has spread out to platforms and ecosystems, and the center of gravity moved with it.

When the organization that runs the CVE program is no longer its biggest contributor, the ground has shifted.

Did you realize MITRE had fallen this far?

#vulnerabilitymanagement #cybersecurity #CVE
```
